Valley Of Tears

Lockdown Time" is a fascinating Israeli war drama series created by Yaron Silberman, based on a script by Ron Leshem, Amit Cohen and Daniel Amsel. The series was broadcast on October 19, 2020 and takes place against the background of the events of the Yom Kippur War. The plot focuses on the northern front of the war. During the series, we will follow a group of soldiers and their command at the end of the Yom Kippur War, at the beginning of 1974. They find themselves in extreme situations and face complex challenges in the environment under the dome. They struggle to maintain life and their perception of the place, while dealing with political, private and personal figures. The series documents the suffering and mental battles of the soldiers during the war, and presents the drama of the war from the point of view of the frontline soldiers. Through the series, we will also explore political, social and personal issues caused by the war and the harsh reality of the army.
